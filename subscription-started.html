What happens now?

It's simple, we'll take $2 from your payment card every month, and in return you get unlimited sites, emails and forms with Howdy up to 1000 email sends per month.

What if I need to cancel?

If you want to cancel your subscription at any point, just drop us an email at support@weareleaf.com and we'll get it cancelled right away. Your paid account will remain active until the end of your current billing month.

When is payment taken?

Payment will be taken every month on the day of the month you started your subscription. The payment should appear as 'HOWDYFORMCOM' on your bank statement.

What if a payment fails?

Don't worry, we won't cut you off! If a payment fails you'll be notified via email along with a link to update your payment details. Failing that, we'll be in touch directly.

What if I exceed my usage allowance?

Again, don't worry, we won't cut you off! We'll be in touch to congratulate you on making such good use of Howdy, and arrange a more payment suitable plan with you.
