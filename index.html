Automagical setup

Installation if foolproof, just paste your generated snippet into your page

Just paste your generated snippet onto the same page as your form HTML, we'll take care of the rest:

  • <script src=“…“ data-howdy-recipient=“your@email.com“ ></script>

You can even re-use the same snippet for different contact forms, just update the data-howdy-recipient attribute on the snippet to have it send to a different inbox:

  • <script src=“…“ data-howdy-recipient=“another@email.com“ ></script>

Input validation has never been so straight-forward

Howdy will choose an appropriate method of validation for your inputs. Alternatively, you can set your own:

  • // Automatically validated:
  • <input type=“text” name=“telephone” />
  • // Set your own validation:
  • <input type=“text” name=“login” data-howdy-validate=“email” />

    • Accepted validation types are none, required, email, number, date, tel.

    Automatically validted input names are email, name, first_name, last_name, firstName, lastName, subject, topic, about, body, message, address, postcode, post_code, postCode, zipcode, zip_code, zipCode, tel, telephone, mobile, mob, age, dob, date_of_birth and dateOfBirth also work.
