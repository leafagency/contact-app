Contact forms a constant pain in the neck for you and your clients? Howdy handles form validation and submission, delivering the results straight to your email inbox. No programming required.

Enter your email address to generate your Howdy code snippet:

Copy & paste this snippet onto the page with your form on to start receiving submissions right to your inbox:

Custom Validation

Validate your custom fields by adding a data-howdy-validate attribute to your input

  • data-howdy-validate=“none” // No validation on this input
  • data-howdy-validate=“required” // Some value is required
  • data-howdy-validate=“email” // Email address is required
  • data-howdy-validate=“number” // A number is required
  • data-howdy-validate=“date” // A date is required
  • data-howdy-validate=“tel” // No letters are allowed
